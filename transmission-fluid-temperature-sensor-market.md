<p><p>The transmission fluid temperature sensor market encompasses various sensor types, each suited for specific applications. Bimetallic temperature sensors utilize two metals to measure heat expansion, while temperature sensor ICs provide accurate electronic readings. Thermistors and resistive temperature detectors (RTDs) measure resistance changes with temperature, offering high precision. Thermocouples generate voltage based on temperature differences, infrared sensors detect thermal radiation without contact, and fiber optic sensors use light for measurement, ensuring safety in harsh environments. Other specialized sensors may cater to niche applications.</p></p>
