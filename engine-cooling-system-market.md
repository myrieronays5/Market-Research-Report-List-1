Internal heat exchangers improve heat transfer between fluids, ensuring effective temperature regulation. Engine coolant pumps circulate coolant throughout the system, preventing overheating. Additionally, various modules integrate these components for simplified installation and operation. Other market offerings may include hoses, thermostats, and sensors that further support engine cooling functionality.</p></p>
