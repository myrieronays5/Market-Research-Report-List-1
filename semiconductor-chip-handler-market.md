<p><p>The semiconductor chip handler market includes various types designed for efficient handling of chips during testing and packaging. Pick-and-place handlers utilize robotic arms to precisely place chips, while turret handlers employ rotating platforms to manage multiple chips simultaneously, optimizing throughput. Gravity handlers rely on gravitational forces for chip placement, offering a simpler, cost-effective solution.
