<p><ul><li>Tubular Wires (Flux-cored and Metal-cored)</li><li>Solid Wires</li><li>Stick Electrodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The filler metals market is categorized into three main types: tubular wires, solid wires, and stick electrodes. Tubular wires include flux-cored and metal-cored options, providing versatility and enhanced performance in various welding applications. Solid wires are commonly used for their ease of handling and high feedability, making them suitable for both manual and automated processes. Stick electrodes, often used in Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW), offer portability and convenience, making them ideal for fieldwork and repair tasks.</p></p>
