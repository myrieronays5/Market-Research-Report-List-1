<p><p>The Non-Fused Safety Switch Disconnector market features two primary types: rotary and knife-blade switches. Rotary switches utilize a rotating mechanism to open or close circuits, providing a compact and user-friendly option for switching applications. In contrast, knife-blade disconnectors have a simple design where a metal blade is inserted into a slot to interrupt the circuit. Both types ensure safety and reliability in electrical systems, allowing for easy maintenance and isolation of equipment from power sources.</p></p>
