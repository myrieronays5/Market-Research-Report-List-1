<p><p>The HVAC controller market encompasses various types designed to optimize indoor climate control. Temperature controllers regulate and maintain desired thermal levels, ensuring comfort and energy efficiency. Humidity controllers monitor and adjust moisture levels to prevent mold and enhance air quality. Ventilation controllers manage the airflow within spaces to ensure proper circulation and improve health standards. Integrated controllers combine functionalities of temperature, humidity, and ventilation controls into a single system, providing comprehensive management and enhanced operational efficiency for HVAC systems.</p></p>
