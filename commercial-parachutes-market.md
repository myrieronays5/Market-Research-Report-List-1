<p><p>The commercial parachutes market comprises several types, including round, square, and cruciform parachutes. Round parachutes are traditional designs primarily used for cargo drops and recreational use. Square parachutes, or ram-air canopies, offer better control and maneuverability, making them popular for skydiving. Cruciform parachutes feature a cross shape that enhances stability and efficiency in various applications, including military and emergency situations. Additionally, the "other" segment includes specialized designs catering to unique operational needs and advancements in parachute technology.</p></p>
