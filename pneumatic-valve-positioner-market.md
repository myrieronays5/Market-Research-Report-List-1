<p><strong>The Pneumatic Valve Positioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioner</li><li>Double Acting Positioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic valve positioner market can be categorized into two main types: single acting and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners utilize a spring to return the valve to its default position when pneumatic pressure is removed, making them simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, double acting positioners use pneumatic pressure in both directions, providing greater control and faster response times, ideal for applications requiring precise regulation. Both types are essential for automation in industries like oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing.</p></p>
