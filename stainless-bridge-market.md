<p><strong>The Stainless Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional</li><li>Cantilever</li><li>Maryland</li><li>Implant-supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless bridge market includes several types catering to different dental needs. Traditional bridges, anchored by adjacent teeth, offer stability but require tooth modification. Cantilever bridges are supported on one side, useful in situations with limited adjacent teeth. Maryland bridges feature a metal framework with wings for bonding to teeth, providing a minimally invasive solution. Implant-supported bridges rely on dental implants for support, offering a durable and aesthetic option, especially in cases of significant tooth loss or damage.</p></p>
