<p><p>The robot pool cleaner market features two primary types: corded and cordless models. In contrast, cordless robot pool cleaners rely on rechargeable batteries, offering greater mobility and easier maneuverability around the pool. While corded options may excel in deep cleaning larger pools, cordless units provide convenience and freedom from tangled cords, catering to different user preferences and pool sizes.</p></p>
