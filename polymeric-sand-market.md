<p><ul><li>Polymeric Sand</li><li>Polymeric Dust</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymeric sand market consists of two primary types: polymeric sand and polymeric dust. Polymeric sand is a fine sand mixed with polymers that, when activated with water, forms a solid bond, reducing weed growth and stabilizing paver joints. It is commonly used in patios, walkways, and driveways. Polymeric dust, a finer alternative, enhances sand joints in hardscaping applications, providing a smooth finish while offering similar benefits such as weed resistance and erosion control. Both products are crucial for durable landscaping solutions.</p></p>
