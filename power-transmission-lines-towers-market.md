<p><ul><li>High Tension (HT)</li><li>Extra High Tension (EHT)</li><li>Ultra High Tension (UHT))</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power transmission lines and towers market comprises various types based on voltage levels, including High Tension (HT), Extra High Tension (EHT), and Ultra High Tension (UHT) systems. HT lines operate between 33 kV and 132 kV, ideal for regional distribution. EHT lines range from 132 kV to 765 kV, suited for long-distance transmission. UHT systems exceed 765 kV, enabling efficient, high-capacity transport of electricity over vast distances, minimizing losses and enhancing grid stability. Each type plays a crucial role in modern energy infrastructure.</p></p>
