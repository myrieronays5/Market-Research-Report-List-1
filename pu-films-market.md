<p><p>The PU films market comprises primarily two types: polyester PU films and polyether PU films. Polyester PU films are known for their superior durability, tensile strength, and resistance to abrasion, making them suitable for applications in automotive and industrial sectors. On the other hand, polyether PU films offer excellent flexibility, hydrolysis resistance, and are often preferred in medical and textile applications. Both types cater to specific demands, driving growth in various industries according to their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
