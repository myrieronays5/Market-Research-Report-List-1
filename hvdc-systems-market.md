<p><p>HVDC systems can be categorized into three main market types: </p><p>1. **Point-to-Point Transmission** connects two locations over long distances, enabling efficient power transfer and grid interconnections. </p><p>2. **Back-to-Back Stations** facilitate direct current interconnections, allowing for the exchange of power between two AC networks without long-distance transmission. </p><p>3. **Multi-Terminal Systems** involve multiple connection points, supporting various sources and loads, enhancing grid flexibility and reliability. These systems collectively advance renewable integration and stability in power networks.</p></p>
