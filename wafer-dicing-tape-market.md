<p><strong>The Wafer Dicing Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Coated Type</li><li>Single Coated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer dicing tape market comprises two main types: double-coated and single-coated types. Double-coated tape features adhesive on both sides, ensuring strong bonding for securing wafers during the dicing process, making it suitable for more demanding applications. In contrast, single-coated tape has adhesive on one side, providing simpler adhesion and easier release, commonly used for less complex dicing tasks. Each type serves distinct needs in semiconductor manufacturing, impacting efficiency and the preservation of wafer integrity during processing.</p></p>
