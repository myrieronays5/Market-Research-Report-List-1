<p><strong>The Thyristors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Thyristor</li><li>Bidirectional Thyristor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thyristor market is categorized into several types: unidirectional thyristors, bidirectional thyristors, and others. Unidirectional thyristors, which allow current to flow in one direction, are commonly used in applications like rectifiers and voltage control. Bidirectional thyristors can conduct current in both directions, making them suitable for AC applications such as light dimmers and motor speed controls. The "others" category includes specialized thyristors like triacs and GTOs (gate-turn-off thyristors), which serve niche applications in power electronics.</p></p>
