<p><p>The proteomic kits market comprises three main types: extraction kits, analysis kits, and sequencing kits. Extraction kits are designed for isolating proteins from biological samples, ensuring purity and integrity for subsequent studies. Analysis kits facilitate the identification and quantification of proteins through various techniques, such as mass spectrometry or ELISA. Sequencing kits enable the comprehensive profiling of proteins and their interactions, providing insights into biological functions and mechanisms. Together, these kits advance research in fields like personalized medicine, drug discovery, and molecular biology.</p></p>
