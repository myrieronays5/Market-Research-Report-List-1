<p><p>The air conditioning expansion valve market includes two main types: Thermal Expansion Valves (TXVs) and Thermostatic Expansion Valves. TXVs regulate refrigerant flow by responding to pressure changes, allowing for precise control of the cooling process. Thermostatic Expansion Valves, a subtype of TXVs, utilize a sensing bulb to monitor evaporator temperature, ensuring consistent cooling efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing air conditioning performance by maintaining the appropriate refrigerant level, enhancing energy efficiency, and improving overall system reliability.</p></p>
