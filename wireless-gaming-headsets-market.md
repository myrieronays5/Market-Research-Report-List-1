<p><p>Wireless gaming headsets utilize various frequency types for communication, including infrared, M Wave, and decimeter wave. Infrared headsets transmit audio signals using light waves, offering limited range but secure connections. M Wave technology operates at microwave frequencies, providing higher bandwidth and reduced latency for immersive gaming experiences over longer distances. Decimeter wave operates at lower frequencies, balancing range and data transmission speed. Each type caters to different user needs, with varying performance in connectivity, range, and sound quality.</p></p>
