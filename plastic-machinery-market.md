<p><p>The plastic machinery market encompasses various types of equipment essential for plastic manufacturing. Injection molding machines are used to produce intricate parts by injecting molten plastic into molds. Extruder machines continuously form plastic by melting and shaping it through a die. Blow molding machines create hollow plastic objects by inflating heated material within a mold. Rolling mill machines are utilized in processing plastic sheets and films. Other machinery includes ancillary equipment, such as granulators and robots, supporting diverse plastic production processes.</p></p>
