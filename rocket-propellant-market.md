<p><p>The rocket propellant market comprises four main types: solid, liquid, gaseous, and hybrid propellants. Solid rocket propellant is characterized by a pre-loaded mixture that burns uniformly, offering simplicity and reliability. Liquid rocket propellant uses two or more fuel components that are mixed and ignited during flight, allowing for precise thrust control. Gaseous propellant involves the combustion of gases, primarily in experimental applications. Hybrid rocket propellant combines solid and liquid fuels for enhanced performance and flexibility, appealing to various space missions.</p></p>
