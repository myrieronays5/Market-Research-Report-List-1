<p><p>The household hair dye market comprises three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes offer short-term color that washes out after a few shampoos, ideal for experimentation. Semi-permanent hair dyes provide longer-lasting color, typically fading over several washes, suitable for those wanting a subtle change without full commitment. Permanent hair dyes offer long-lasting results, with color that endures until hair grows out or is cut, making them popular for complete transformations.</p></p>
