<p><ul><li>Vertical Furnace</li><li>Horizontal Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor diffusion furnace market is divided into vertical and horizontal furnace types. Vertical furnaces feature a chamber where wafers are processed in a vertical orientation, allowing for effective gas flow and uniform temperature distribution, making them suitable for high-volume production. Horizontal furnaces, on the other hand, have a chamber positioned horizontally, providing easy handling of wafers but often requiring more precise temperature control. Both designs cater to different manufacturing processes and performance requirements within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
