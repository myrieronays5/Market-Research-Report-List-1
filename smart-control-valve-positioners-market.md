<p><strong>The Smart Control Valve Positioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioners</li><li>Double Acting Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart control valve positioners market comprises two main types: single acting and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners utilize a spring mechanism to adjust the valve position, providing control in one direction. They are often simpler and more cost-effective. Conversely, double acting positioners employ pressure to control the valve in both directions, allowing for greater precision and response time. This makes them suitable for more complex applications requiring accurate flow control and robust performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
