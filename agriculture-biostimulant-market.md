<p><p>The agriculture biostimulant market is primarily divided into two types: natural and biosynthetic. Natural biostimulants are derived from organic sources such as plants, microbes, and animal materials, enhancing soil fertility and plant health through natural processes. In contrast, biosynthetic biostimulants are chemically formulated and designed to mimic natural products, targeting specific plant responses to improve growth and resilience. Both types aim to enhance crop productivity while promoting sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
