<p><p>The gun sights market is primarily divided into two main types: optical and mechanical sights. Optical sights utilize lenses and reticles to enhance target acquisition and accuracy, commonly seen in scopes and red dot sights, making them popular for hunting and competitive shooting. Mechanical sights, including iron sights, rely on physical alignment of front and rear sight components for aiming, offering simplicity and reliability. Each type caters to different user preferences and shooting scenarios, influencing their design and functionality.</p></p>
